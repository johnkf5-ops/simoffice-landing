Instant Streaming Chat

Major

The entire chat engine has been rewritten from the ground up. Responses stream instantly as agents type — no more waiting for full replies. Zero duplicate messages. The chat store was rebuilt from 2,078 lines down to 610 — faster, cleaner, and rock solid.

💬

Team Chat Rooms

Major

Create rooms with multiple agents. Team mode lets all agents respond in sequence — each sees what prior agents said. @Mention mode targets one agent. Both modes share the same feed. Human-gated: agents never loop.

🎨

Agent Customization

New

Upload custom avatars, pick from 10 colors, and rename your agents. Make your team yours. Customizations persist across sessions.

File Upload in Chat

New

Drop files directly into any conversation. Your agent reads, analyzes, and responds to documents, images, spreadsheets, and code files. Drag-and-drop or click to upload.

iMessage Integration

New

Your AI agents now work over iMessage. Text your agent from your iPhone, iPad, or Mac — they respond in real time. Set up in minutes with the step-by-step wizard. No third-party apps required.
