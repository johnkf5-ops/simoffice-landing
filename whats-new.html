Performance

Every agent personality file has been trimmed — 50% smaller on average. Removed bloated example dialogues that wasted tokens without improving performance. Your agents now load faster and respond with the same quality using less context.

Free Local AI

Major

Run AI entirely on your Mac — no cloud, no API keys, no monthly fees. SimOffice auto-detects your hardware, recommends the best model, downloads and installs Ollama, and pulls a model — all through a guided wizard. 16 GB+ Apple Silicon recommended. Your data never leaves your machine.

Smart Hardware Detection

New

SimOffice scans your RAM, chip type, and available disk space, then recommends the best local AI model for your machine. 8 GB Macs get guided to cloud providers instead. No guesswork, no crashes from running a model that's too big.

The update button now does what it says. Check for updates, download, and install — all from Settings. Plus a new auto-update toggle that downloads and installs updates automatically so you're always current.
