v1.1 Release

What's new in
SimOffice.

We've been busy. Here's everything that shipped — new features, integrations, and quality-of-life improvements.

🏢

3D Virtual Office

Major

Your AI team now works in a full 3D office. Watch agents walk around, sit at desks, and collaborate in real time. Assign agents to specific desks with edit mode. It's not a dashboard — it's an office that feels alive.

🤖

177 Pre-Built AI Agents

Major

Browse a full catalog of agents — sales reps, support agents, marketers, finance analysts, HR assistants, developers, and more. Each agent comes with a unique SOUL.md personality file that shapes how they think and respond. Filter by category, search by name, or browse the popular picks.

🎯

Career-Based Onboarding

New

New onboarding flow: pick your career (Marketing, Sales, Engineering, etc.) and we'll recommend a pre-built team of AI agents tailored to your work. Name your office, connect your AI provider, and you're running in under 2 minutes.

🔗

29 Business Integrations

Major

Your agents can now connect to the tools you actually use — and take action, not just chat. Manage your CRM. Send proposals. Track invoices. Post on Slack. Create Jira tickets. All automated.

Works With Any AI

New

Works with any LLM — Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Grok, Ollama, and anything else with an API. Each provider has curated model dropdowns so you pick the right model for your budget and speed needs. Bring your own API key or run locally — your data never touches our servers.

💬

Messaging Channel Wizards

New

Step-by-step setup wizards for Discord, Telegram, WhatsApp, iMessage, and Signal. No more copy-pasting config files. Click through the wizard, scan a QR code or paste a token, and your agents are live in your messaging apps.

🗨️

Buddy List Chat

New

Chat with any agent right from the buddy list. Switch between agents instantly. Every agent has a unique personality powered by their SOUL.md file — so your sales agent talks differently than your support agent.

⚡

Skills & Automations

New

Give your agents new abilities with skills, and set up cron-based automations so work happens on a schedule. Daily reports, weekly summaries, hourly checks — your agents work even when you're not watching.

🔄

Auto-Updates

Quality of life

SimOffice now checks for updates automatically and installs them in the background. You'll always be on the latest version without lifting a finger.

🍎

Native macOS App

Platform

Signed, notarized, and ready to go. Runs on Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) and Intel. Distributed as a DMG — drag to Applications and you're done. Windows coming soon.
